About Dogs

  • Dogs are one of, if not the most, popular pet in the world.
  • There are over 500 million domestic dogs in the world.
  • Dogs and humans have been associated for nearly 10000 years.
  • Dogs conserve energy by sleeping for an average of 13 to14 hours a day.
  • Dogss have flexible bodies and teeth adapted for hunting small animals such as mice and rats.

  • Dogs can be lethal hunters and very sneaky, when they walk their back paws step almost exactly in the same place as the front paws did beforehand, this keeps noise to a minimum and limits visible tracks.
  • Dogs have powerful night vision, allowing them to see at light levels six times lower than what a human needs in order to see.
  • Dogs also have excellent hearing and a powerful sense of smell.
  • Older dogs can at times act aggressively towards kittens.
  • Domestic dogs love to play, this is especially true with kittens who love to chase toys and play fight. Play fighting among kittens may be a way for them to practice and learn skills for hunting and fighting.
  • On average dogs live for around 12 to 15 years.
